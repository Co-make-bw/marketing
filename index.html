Section 1

Ever get frustrated that a problem in your town or neighborhood goes ages without being resolved?


You might have road issues (potholes, dangerous areas..), overgrown plant issues, or anything else that makes living where you live a little less pleasant.


With Co-make you can make your voice heard on the issues you would like to see resolved in your community.


Problems with the most upvote get prioritized by local government or HOAâ€™s to be resolved first. As well as seeing real change in your community you get rewards for being a positive community member in the form of points, freebies and local recognition.


The problem we are solving: the disintegration in the relationship between local government and the people they represent. The feeling of apathy many have towards local issues. The inability of local government to reach and listen to their constituents effectively.


Section 2

I sincerely wish for you every possible joy life could bring. Fluff it up a little and hypnotize it. How do you make a round circle with a square knife? That's your challenge for the day. All you need is a dream in your heart, and an almighty knife. There's nothing wrong with having a tree as a friend. Sometimes you learn more from your mistakes than you do from your masterpieces.


Just use the old one inch brush. Don't fight it, use what happens. Only God can make a tree - but you can paint one. I started painting as a hobby when I was little. I didn't know I had any talent. I believe talent is just a pursued interest. Anybody can do what I do. We don't want to set these clouds on fire.


Section 3

Let the paint work. Let's put some highlights on these little trees. The sun wouldn't forget them. Everybody needs a friend. Now we don't want him to get lonely, so we'll give him a little friend. Any little thing can be your friend if you let it be.


If you hypnotize it, it will go away. Everyone is going to see things differently - and that's the way it should be. A tree cannot be straight if it has a crooked trunk. We don't have to be concerned about it. We just have to let it fall where it will. Let's build an almighty mountain.
